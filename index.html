About Code नक्षत्र

Welcome to Code नक्षत्र, the ultimate 24-hour hackathon hosted by Geek Room TIIPS and Code Rangers on 7 & 8 March 2025 at TIIPS, Greater Noida, where innovation thrives and boundaries are shattered!

This high-energy event is more than just a competition; it’s an adrenaline-fueled journey where coding prowess meets imaginative thinking. Bringing together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts, Code नक्षत्र serves as a beacon of excellence, fostering collaboration, creativity, and groundbreaking problem-solving.

Participants will tackle real-world challenges, attend expert-led workshops, gain mentorship, and compete for exciting prizes, internships, and networking opportunities. Beyond the thrill of competition, this hackathon is a launchpad for visionary ideas, driving technological advancements and shaping the future. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a first-time hacker, this is your chance to showcase your skills, push the limits of innovation, and make an impact. Join us as we revolutionize the tech landscape and propel your brand to new heights of visibility and success!
